This mini paper explains the fundamental operation of a Boost Converter simulated in PSIM.  
A 25 V input is converted to 50 V output under open-loop PWM control.  
The focus is on how inductance, capacitance, and duty ratio affect output voltage, ripple, and transient behavior.
